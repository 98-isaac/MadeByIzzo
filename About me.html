About Me

My Journey in Tech and Music

Hello! I'm Isaac Didison Amule Aka Dj Izzo, a creative professional with a passion for both technology and music. With over 5 years of experience in the industry, I've blended my skills in DJing, design, and web development to create unique experiences for clients and audiences alike.

My journey began in college where I studied information technology while DJing at local events. After graduation, I worked as a network administrator while building my design skills through freelance projects. Today, I balance my time between DJ performances, creative design work, and web development projects.

I believe that technology and creativity are not mutually exclusive – in fact, they complement each other beautifully. This philosophy guides all of my work, whether I'm designing a website, spinning tracks at an event, or setting up a network infrastructure.
